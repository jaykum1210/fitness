Exercise Breakdown

Back Squat

ü¶µ Quads & Glutes 4 sets √ó 5-8 reps ‚ö° Compound

The ultimate lower body exercise! Builds massive strength in quads, glutes, and core while improving functional movement patterns and athletic performance.

üìã Instructions

  1. Position barbell across upper back, feet shoulder-width apart
  2. Keep chest up, core braced, and back straight
  3. Lower down by pushing hips back and bending knees
  4. Descend until thighs are parallel to floor or lower
  5. Drive through heels to return to standing position

üí° Tips

  • Keep knees tracking over toes, not caving inward
  • Maintain neutral spine throughout the movement
  • Start with lighter weight to perfect your form

Romanian Deadlift

ü¶µ Hamstrings & Glutes 3 sets √ó 8-10 reps ‚ö° Compound

Excellent for building posterior chain strength and improving hip hinge movement. Targets hamstrings, glutes, and lower back while enhancing flexibility.

üìã Instructions

  1. Stand with feet hip-width apart, barbell at thighs
  2. Hinge at hips, pushing them back
  3. Lower barbell while keeping it close to legs
  4. Feel stretch in hamstrings at bottom
  5. Drive hips forward to return to standing

üí° Tips

  • Keep barbell close to your legs throughout
  • Don't round your back - maintain neutral spine
  • Focus on the hip hinge, not squatting down

Builds unilateral strength and stability while targeting quads, glutes, and core. Great for improving balance and functional movement patterns.

üìã Instructions

  1. Stand with feet together, core engaged
  2. Step forward into a lunge position
  3. Lower back knee toward ground
  4. Push through front heel to step forward
  5. Alternate legs for desired reps

üí° Tips

  • Keep your torso upright throughout
  • Step far enough forward to create 90-degree angles
  • Don't let your front knee cave inward

The ultimate glute builder! Targets glutes, hamstrings, and core while improving hip extension power and posterior chain strength.

üìã Instructions

  1. Sit with upper back against bench, barbell on hips
  2. Drive through heels to lift hips up
  3. Squeeze glutes at the top

  5. Keep core engaged throughout

üí° Tips

  • Focus on squeezing your glutes at the top
  • Keep your chin tucked to avoid hyperextension
  • Use a pad on the barbell for comfort

ü¶µ Calves 3 sets √ó 12-15 reps ‚ö° Isolation

Builds strong, defined calves while improving ankle stability and lower leg strength. Essential for complete lower body development.

üìã Instructions

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
  2. Hold dumbbells at sides or use calf raise machine

  4. Squeeze calves at the top
  5. Lower with control to starting position

üí° Tips

  • Get a full stretch at the bottom
  • Hold the contraction at the top
  • Control the negative portion of the movement
