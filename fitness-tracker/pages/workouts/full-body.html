Barbell Back Squat

The king of all exercises! Targets quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings, and core. Builds functional strength and power while improving mobility and stability.

üìã Instructions

  1. Position barbell across upper back, feet shoulder-width apart
  2. Keep chest up, core braced, and back straight

Excellent chest builder that also targets shoulders and triceps. Dumbbells allow for greater range of motion and unilateral strength development.

üìã Instructions

  1. Lie on bench with dumbbells at chest level
  2. Press dumbbells up until arms are fully extended
  3. Lower with control until chest is stretched
  4. Keep shoulder blades retracted throughout

üí° Tips

üìã Instructions

The ultimate core exercise that builds stability, strength, and endurance. Engages the entire core musculature including deep stabilizers.

üìã Instructions

üí° Tips
