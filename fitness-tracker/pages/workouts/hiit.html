Interval Protocol

‚è±Ô∏è 30s Work üò¥ 60s Rest üîÑ 12 Rounds

The perfect HIIT protocol for maximum fat burning and cardiovascular improvement. Short, intense bursts followed by adequate recovery.

üìã Workout Structure

  1. Warm-up: 5 minutes light cardio + dynamic stretches
  2. Work Interval: 30 seconds maximum effort
  3. Rest Interval: 60 seconds active recovery
  4. Repeat: 12 rounds total
  5. Cool-down: 5 minutes light cardio + stretching

üí° Intensity Guidelines

  • Work intervals should be 8-9/10 perceived exertion
  • Rest intervals should be 3-4/10 perceived exertion
  • You should be able to speak in short phrases during work
  • Recovery should feel easy and controlled

Exercise Options

Sprints

üèÉ Running üî• High Impact ‚ö° Maximum Power

The ultimate HIIT exercise! Sprints engage your entire body and burn maximum calories while building explosive power and speed.

üìã Instructions

  1. Find a flat surface or use a treadmill
  2. Start with a light jog to warm up
  3. Sprint at maximum effort for 30 seconds
  4. Walk or jog slowly for 60 seconds recovery
  5. Repeat for all 12 rounds

üí° Tips

  • Focus on quick, powerful strides
  • Keep your core engaged throughout
  • Use proper running form to prevent injury

Burpees

üí™ Full Body üî• Bodyweight ‚ö° High Intensity

The ultimate full-body exercise! Burpees combine strength and cardio in one explosive movement that targets every major muscle group.

üìã Instructions

  1. Start standing, then squat down
  2. Jump back into plank position
  3. Do a push-up (optional)
  4. Jump feet back to squat position
  5. Jump up with arms overhead

üí° Tips

  • Modify by stepping instead of jumping
  • Skip the push-up if needed
  • Focus on smooth, controlled movements

Mountain Climbers

üî• Core & Cardio üí™ Bodyweight ‚ö° High Intensity

Excellent for core strength and cardiovascular fitness. Mountain climbers engage your entire body while improving coordination and agility.

üìã Instructions

  1. Start in plank position
  2. Bring one knee toward chest
  3. Quickly switch legs
  4. Continue alternating at high speed
  5. Keep core engaged throughout

üí° Tips

  • Keep your hips level with your shoulders
  • Focus on speed and control
  • Don't let your hips sag or pike up

Jump Squats

ü¶µ Lower Body üî• Plyometric ‚ö° Explosive Power

Build explosive lower body power while getting your heart rate up. Jump squats combine strength and cardio for maximum efficiency.

üìã Instructions

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
  2. Squat down until thighs are parallel to floor
  3. Explosively jump up as high as possible
  4. Land softly and immediately go into next squat
  5. Repeat for the full 30 seconds

üí° Tips

  • Land softly to protect your joints
  • Keep your chest up throughout
  • Use your arms to help with the jump

üîÑ HIIT Variations

Different Protocols

  • Beginner: 20s work / 40s rest (easier to start)
  • Intermediate: 30s work / 60s rest (standard protocol)
  • Advanced: 40s work / 20s rest (more challenging)
  • Tabata: 20s work / 10s rest (maximum intensity)

üí° Equipment Alternatives

  • Stationary Bike: Great for beginners, low impact
  • Rowing Machine: Full body workout, excellent for HIIT
  • Battle Ropes: Upper body focused, high intensity
  • Elliptical: Low impact, good for joint issues
  • Stair Climber: Lower body focused, high calorie burn
